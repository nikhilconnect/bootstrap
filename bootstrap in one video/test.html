    Our Story


    Enactus is an international non-profit organisation and the largest experiential learning platform that connects young minds, experienced veterans and marginalized communities through entrepreneurial-based projects and is dedicated to create a better world while developing the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders.

    Enactus Ramjas, established in 2011, is a student-run organisation that seeks to uplift communities through entrepreneurial action. Under expert guidance, the student leaders at Enactus engage in the formation and implementation of community development programmes.

    In the 11 years since its inception, Enactus Ramjas has been decorated with numerous grants from KPMG, Godrej, Walmart, Mahindra, etc. and various achievements, while gaining national recognition.

    We have worked on 10+ projects viz Aarogya, Aradhaga, Khwab, Swabun, TransCreations, to name a few, created 182+ entrepreneurs and impacted thousands of lives. With numerous communities and countless volunteer hours, we move towards a single goal of ours: a better world.

    Because, change is always from within for what's around!

    Our Mission and Goals

    Zero Hunger

    To end hunger, all forms of malnutrition and ensure access of nutritious, safe and sufficient food throughout the year.

    Good Health and Well Being

    To ensure healthy lives and promote well being for all at all ages.

    Reduced Inequalities

    To uplift marginalized communities both economically and socially through an entrepreneurial effort.

    Clean Water and Sanitation

    To achieve universal, equitable, and adequate access to safe and affordable drinking water, sanitation and hygiene for all.

    Responsible Production and Consumption

    To reduce future environmental & social costs and strengthen economic competitiveness.

    Gender Equality

    To provide women and girls with equal access to health care, decent work and other facilities and achieve sustainable economies.
